Notice that in the first table, \(45/55\text{,}\) or \(82\%\text{,}\) of White voters voted for the Heart (\(\heartsuit \)) candidate while only \(15/45 = 33 \%\) of non-White voters voted for the Heart candidate, suggesting that there is a significant difference in the voting patterns by racial group(s). The second table tells a different story: \(30/55 = 55 \%\) of White voters voted for the Heart candidate and \(30/45 = 67 \%\) of non-White voters voted for the Heart candidate.
