Finally, the Convex Hull score is a ratio that measures how “convex” a district shape is. In geometry, a convex shape is one in which any two points in the interior of the shape can be connected by a straight line that stays inside the shape. If there is any pair of interior points connected by a straight line that partially falls outside of the shape, then the shape is known as concave.
