Item 6.b.v.

Now, our goal is to get all our \(R^{2}\) values larger than roughly 0.96, which is a very large degree of accuracy. If some of your trendlines have \(R^{2}\leq0.96\text{,}\) double-click on the offending trendline(s) until the sidebar comes up and increase the Order of your polynomial until \(R^{2}>0.96\text{.}\)
