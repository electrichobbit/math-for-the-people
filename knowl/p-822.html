Consider using the following prompts with generative AI, such as Chat GPT, to develop additional exercises for this material. These programs can also be used to generate solutions, but those solutions will not necessarily be correct! These prompts have been tested with ChatGPT [6.11.1.117].

  • Give me 10 math problems about simple interest and payday lending that encourage students to engage with the economic disparities of payday lending.

  • Give me 10 math problems about calculating the expected value of a payday loan for the lender. Focus on the challenges payday loans create for individuals and communities.

  • I've given my students a file which contains data on the number of predatory lending institutions in each county, as well as data on race, income, and education. Give me 10 problems which use correlation to help students uncover patterns in the data and how predatory lending institutions are distributed.
