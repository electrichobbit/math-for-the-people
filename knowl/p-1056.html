The United States is a relative outlier compared to other nations in the high cost of college tuition and fees. For example, according to a late \(2017\) study by the Organisation for Economic Cooperation and Development (OECD), the U.S. has the highest average tuition costs of the \(35\) OECD member countries [9.11.167]. This tuition has increased over time.
