Computing a figure per 100,000 people in an area is a common way to compare statistics in areas of differing populations.

Calculate the correlation to see if larger counties do have more payday lenders. Then calculate the correlation between the number of payday lenders per 100,000 people and the percentage of each race in that county to see if there is still a correlation after we adjust for population.
