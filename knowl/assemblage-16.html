The Payday Lending industry in the USA had 11.2 billion dollars of revenue in 2019[6.11.1.100]. These companies benefit directly from the profits of payday lending, but they are not the only ones. A 2011 report by the Public Accountability Initiative found that payday lenders obtain much of their financing from major banks like Wells Fargo, JP Morgan Chase, and Bank of America [6.11.1.99]. They also found that the payday lenders had ties to many of the large Wall Street investment banks, either through partial ownership or through shared personnel.
