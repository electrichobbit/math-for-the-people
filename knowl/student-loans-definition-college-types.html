Definition 9.4.4. For-Profit and Not-for-Profit Colleges.

All public and most private colleges (\(91\%\) of all colleges in \(2016\) [9.11.171]) are not-for-profit institutions, meaning they invest their profits back into the institution and often operate under independent governance.

A college that is not registered as a not-for-profit institution is called a for-profit college.
