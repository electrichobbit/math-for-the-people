Exercise 10.

For each statement, explain why the statement is not an appropriate interpretation of compactness scores.

  1. The average Polsby-Popper score of districts in Maryland is 0.115, thus the districts are not compact.

  3. The average Polsby-Popper score of New Jersey's congressional districts is 0.20 while the average Polsby-Popper score of Pennsylvania's congressional districts is 0.33, thus Pennsylvania is more compact.
